Kenyan Ugali

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 4 cups water
  • Pinch of salt (optional)

Traditional Method

  1. Bring water to boil in a sufuria
  2. Gradually add maize flour while stirring continuously
  3. Keep stirring until thick (5-7 minutes)
  4. Cover and let simmer for 3 minutes
  5. Shape into a round mound and serve hot

Serving Tradition

Ugali is Kenya's staple food, best enjoyed with sukuma wiki, nyama choma, or any stew.
