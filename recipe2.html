Kenyan Pilau

Ingredients (serves 6)

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g beef (cubed)
  • 3 cups beef stock
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tbsp pilau masala
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Cooking Process

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes, then drain
  2. Heat oil in pot and caramelize onions (10 mins)
  3. Add garlic and spices, cook until fragrant
  4. Brown beef on all sides, add Â½ cup stock and simmer until tender (30 mins)
  5. Add remaining stock and bring to boil
  6. Stir in rice, reduce heat to lowest setting
  7. Cover tightly and cook for 20 minutes without opening
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving

Serving Tradition

Pilau is a celebratory dish in Kenya, often served at weddings, holidays, and special occasions with kachumbari.
